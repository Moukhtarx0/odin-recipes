Pizza

Description

Pizza is a globally popular Italian dish featuring a flattened, leavened bread base topped with savory ingredients, most commonly tomato sauce and cheese (typically mozzarella), baked at high temperatures.

Ingredients

  • Dough (Base): Typically made from all-purpose or bread flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil. Some recipes add sugar for yeast activation or cornmeal for a crisp texture on the bottom.
  • Sauce: The standard is a tomato-based sauce, often enhanced with crushed tomatoes, garlic, onion, olive oil, and dried herbs like basil or oregano. White pizzas may use Alfredo or olive oil instead of tomato sauce.
  • Cheese: Low-moisture mozzarella is preferred for melting, though fresh mozzarella is common for Neapolitan styles. Other popular cheeses include parmesan, provolone, fontina, gorgonzola, and goat cheese.
  • Toppings (Optional): Sliced onions, mushrooms, bell peppers, black olives, pineapple, and spinach

Steps

    Prepare and Rise the Dough
  1. Activate Yeast: Mix warm water with yeast and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for about 5 to 10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Mix and Knead: Combine the yeast mixture with flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead the dough by hand for 8 to 10 minutes or in a mixer for 7 to 10 minutes until it is smooth and elastic.
  3. First Rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it with a damp towel, and let it rise in a warm spot for 1 to 2 hours until it doubles in size.
    4. Shape and Top
  4. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to its highest setting typically 450째F to 500째F (230째C to 260째C). If using a pizza stone, preheat it for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Stretch the Dough: On a floured surface, gently press and stretch the dough into a circle about 10 to 12 inches wide. Avoid using a rolling pin if you want a puffier crust.
  6. Add Toppings: Spread a thin layer of sauce, leaving a 1-inch border for the crust. Add cheese and your chosen toppings. Be careful not to overload the pizza, or it may become soggy.
    7. Bake and Serve
  7. Bake: Slide the pizza onto a preheated stone or baking tray. Bake for 8 to 15 minutes depending on the temperature, until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
  8. Finish: Let the pizza rest for 2 to 3 minutes before slicing to allow the cheese to set.
