Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

The gravy often appears glossy, orange-red, or deep orange, garnished with fresh green coriander leaves and a swirl of cream. It is usually served in a shallow bowl alongside fluffy white basmati rice or flatbreads like naan.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cubed.
  • Yogurt: Plain yogurt (Greek or natural)
  • Spices: Which include ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric and chili
  • Onions: Red Onion
  • Tomato: Tomato purée
  • Coconut milk: Any brand
  • Fresh cilantro: For garnish

Steps

    Marinate the chicken
  1. Prepare Chicken: Cut the chicken into inch bite-sized cubes
  2. Mix Marinade: In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and spices (garam masala, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder).
  3. Combine & Chill: Toss the chicken pieces in the marinade until fully coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, though 8 to 24 hours is best for the most tender results.
    4. Cook the "Tikka" (Chicken)
  4. Char the Meat: For a traditional smoky flavour, thread the chicken onto skewers and grill or broil at a high heat (approx. 230°C/450°F) for 10 to 15 minutes until slightly charred on the edges.
  5. Alternatives: You can also sauté the chicken in a hot pan with a little oil until browned on all sides. It does not need to be fully cooked through yet, as it will finish in the sauce.
    6. Prepare the Masala Sauce
  6. Sauté Aromatics: Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add finely chopped onions and cook for 5 to 10 minutes until soft and golden. Stir in more ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  7. Toast Spices: Stir in the sauce spices (cumin, coriander, paprika, and garam masala) and toast for 30 to 60 seconds until fragrant.
  8. Simmer Base: Pour in the tomato purée or chopped tomatoes. Let the sauce simmer on medium-low heat for 10 to 15 minutes until it thickens and the oil begins to separate from the sauce.
    9. Combine and Finish
  9. Add Chicken: Stir the grilled or sautéed chicken pieces (and any resting juices) into the simmering sauce.
  10. Make it Creamy: Stir in the heavy cream and a pinch of sugar (if needed to balance the acidity). Simmer for an additional 5 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  11. Garnish: Stir in crushed dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) and top with fresh coriander. Serve hot with basmati rice or naan.
